Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 20, 2026

RajPay Calc (the "App") is a utility application designed to help Rajasthan Government employees calculate their estimated salary. This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do NOT collect, store, or share any personal information.

  • Local Calculation: All salary calculations are performed locally on your device. The data you input (such as basic pay, department, or allowances) never leaves your device and is not stored on any external servers.
  • No Personal Identifiable Information (PII): We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any other identification details.

2. Permissions

The App requires minimal permissions to function:

  • Internet Access: Used solely for checking for app updates or loading core libraries.
  • Strict Privacy: We do NOT access your location, camera, microphone, contacts, or storage files.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses standard framework libraries (like Expo/React Native) to ensure performance. These do not collect personal data through our App. We do not use any tracking or analytics services.

4. Data Safety

Since the App does not collect any data, your privacy is protected by design. No data is transmitted to us or any third party.

Disclaimer: The calculations provided by RajPay Calc are for informational and estimation purposes only. Users are advised to verify their final salary with their respective DDO (Drawing and Disbursing Officer).
